Are You Truly Seeing??

It's a question and I put it to you, Are you truly seeing what you ought to see, or you are just looking? Are you truly seeing the things that can be, or you are just seeing the things that are? Are you truly seeing what lies beneath, Beyond the physical or you are only looking at the things that surrounds you? Are you truly seeing the long perfected reality of you, right from the beginning or you just choose to see what you want? There's a seeing that goes beyond you, Farther than your eyes can look, It is VISION and not sight. Your eyes were given you not just for sight, But in alignment with your heart for vision, Your heart( mind) captures the vision and your eyes sees it's reality. Don't limit your heart (mind) and eye, They should work together, For you to become all that God designed you to be..... Your purpose for living!!
