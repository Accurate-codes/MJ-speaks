Now You Can't Run Away!

. . . You have always wanted to hide, From everyone and the world at large, You learnt so many skills, And acquired quite a lot of them, To enable you keep your pace, And run so hard... When people say, "you can't hide from God," You laugh it out, And never bother thinking about it, Because you believe it's only a joke. You have so learnt to want to serve God in your own way, behind the scene, Where no one can ever recognize you, Just you doing your thing and enjoying God, In a way only you understand.... How you thought and wished this could go on, Being cool with everything as you trust God for help, even when storms of life comes, His unending protection is over you, His Mercy ever renewed every morning over you, You bask in his unfailing love, Without any cares of life, What a beautiful one indeed.... But alas, it has all come to an end, Now He says, "you can't hide or run anymore , You can't secretly enjoy me all to yourself, Because I want you out there to represent Me, I am pushing you out......" "You cried and pleaded, "please don't", Don't do this to me now, I don't want to go out, I don't know anything outside you..... " How will I cope? Where do I start from? These and many more were the questions that plagued your fragile heart... Then He spoke, It's time dearly beloved, It's time to face your fears and the world, You can't keep shying away, I am with you always, And I will go with you, I will be your rereward, And your defense, You are never alone, hold on to me, And watch me take you through this journey, My plans for you are of good and not of evil, You are forever loved dear little oneðŸŒ¹ The Lord is with you!
