Wisdom Beckons!!

All you ever wanted was to learn to do things rightly, Not to please yourself or your friends, But to please the Lord. You thought you have been doing it right all this while, Doing the little things that seems right to people, But are you truly living right? Do you really know what you are doing? You can't answer this question, Because you have been a fool all along, Deceiving your own self, Thinking you are wise. Is this truly wisdom you were taught, Or foolishness you acquired from your friends? But it looks like you have been diligent enough, Only to discover that you have been diligently doing and believing the wrong things... What exactly am I supposed to do, That is actually the right thing? Is it going out with friends, Asking beautiful ladies out, Showing off as an handsome guy or a pretty young lady? Oh! foolishness and mediocrity it is.... Your word says, " Teach us to number our days, that we may apply our heart unto wisdom", I truly want to walk and live wisely, And not as a fool anymore, I truly want to know the right thing to do each day, And not overwhelm myself with vain activities which profit nothing, I truly want to know your Will for my life, And live by it, Without relenting. Please help me Lord!
