GOD IS TRUSTWORTHY!

I know you might want to doubt this but it's the truth. He is not a man like you and I that He should lie or fail. He cannot lie to you, or has He? Then why doubt Him and His word? why think of Him as a scam... He will never defraud you like man will, He is too big to stoop so low as do that. Have you ever wondered why some people still keep trusting Him even till their last breathe? It is because He has been tested and proven true countless times, and He has a track record of trustworthiness. Apostle Paul said "Though He slay me, yet will I follow Him", what a profound statement! Have you stopped trusting Him? I know times are hard right now but His word says " He is working out things for your good". Don't stop now, out of this ashes, He is bringing out something beautiful and wonderful. It's just a matter of time. It may not look like it now but, it will wow you later. Just patiently wait and stay with Him, He is indeed faithful to the end.
