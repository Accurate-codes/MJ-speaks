Are There Still True Worshippers?

It saddens my heart these days when I go to church and see people lift up their hands in worship, when it's not genuinely from their hearts. I wonder to myself, are there still true worshippers who worship the Father in sincerity of heart?, who are truly overwhelmed by the father's love and not pretend or make a display of outward worship when the heart is far from Him. Is it not the Bible that says in John 4:23-24 " But the hour cometh, and now is, when the true worshippers shall worship the Father in spirit and in truth: for the Father seeketh such to worship him. God is a Spirit: and they that worship him must worship him in spirit and in truth". So, it's supposed to be Now that the true worshippers will worship the Father in spirit and truth not in the future. What could have gone wrong? oh! indeed it is the last days, where the love of many has gone cold because, iniquity has abound. People don't really understand anymore, the true meaning of worship. They don't know that, it's a lifestyle and not an act. How I wish that we would come back to the way it use to be and stand firmly on it. However, you can decide to come back to that life of true worship and be that difference that the world could look up to. It is a personal decision and not a collective one, so, choose to be that true worshipper in heart and character, lifting the banner of Jesus high.
