Brace Up!!

There's more to you and ahead of you, Don't let the present moment weaken you, And kill the vision He has given you right from the beginning, It could have been someone else, But He decides to choose you, A weakling, One whom the world sees as a babe, One without strength or might. He said He wants you, Not because you could do it, But because He is willing and ready to do it through you, He wants to hold your frail and shaky hands, And guide you as you take each steps as He leads you, He knows how imperfect you are, And how fragile you are, But He is excited to walk down the aisle with you, not minding what the world thinks, Though you are still afraid, He said "do not be scared", I am with you and will always be with you, That's His promises, And He is committed to keeping it, As long as you allow Him lead you. You are dearly loved by Abba!
