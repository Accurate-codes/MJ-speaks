Are you still wondering??

You have been wondering for days now, "why does it seems like God is far from me?" What exactly happened and who created the gap between you and your maker?... Sin has done this harm to you and, mind you,it is still planning to do you more harm........... Sin pulls you far away from your Father and rubs you off your access to the Father, I mean your Heavenly father. Could you recall how it came to you sweetly and unnoticed? it appeared harmless and subtly, making you see no wrong in it. Have you forgotten how it was painted to Eve in the most beautiful manner without any suspicion? So also the devil keeps painting it to look normal and alluring, with no intent of harm. Don't be deceived, what is wrong, is wrong no matter how they try to paint it to you. Always stand for what is right cause that's what Jesus will do if He were to be in your shoes. Do not neglect sin, don't be complacent, you are not as strong as you think you are, take heed and flee. Daily yield and submit to the leading of the Holy Spirit, then you can be assured of help. The Bible says " He that thinks he stands should take heed, less he falls". Jesus loves you❤️❤️ #MJSpeaks
