Have You Found Him Yet??

There's a vaccum in your heart that only God can fill. No one or anything can fill that space except Him. The world itself is full of confusion and chaos, people are truly in need of something beyond the human realm that they can totally surrender to and be rest assured for life. This can only be God, your creator and no one else. Please find God, He is closer to you than you can imagine. He said in his word "Call upon me and I will answer thee and show you great and mighty things which you don't know". You are a mystery wrapped up in a body and to unravel the mystery of You, you need GOD!!
